Suzanne Collins

Suzanne Collins is the author of the immensely popular Hunger Games series of young-adult novels,which has become one of the best selling novels of all time.The series has more than 100 million copies inprint worldwide, and spent more than 260 consecutive weeks on The Times best-seller list. The books have spawned four record-breaking films and many Katniss Halloween costumes.
